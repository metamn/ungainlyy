How much a website cost?

In order to answer this delicate question let's first analyse what a website means in deliverables, in the classic way.

Folyo published a recent survey which helps us examine pricing:

  1. A logo — $500 - $2,500
  2. The design itself in Photoshop or wireframes — $1,000 - $5,000
  3. The design for the mobile site — $1,000 - $5,000
  4. The backend application, the so called Content Management System (blog). The designs above adapted to web by web developers — $50 hourly rate * 6 days of work = $3,000

Adding up the averages you'll have a $10,000 bill at the end.
Which is quite okay verified through the global rates.
