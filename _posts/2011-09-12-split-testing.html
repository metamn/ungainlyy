You and me might have ideas what works best for your customers.
But let them make the final decision!

Split testing presents two or more versions of the same website in a transparent way for visitors, for a short period of time.
Meantime in the background it measures which version is more effective to reach certain goals, like purchase and subscribe.
Then the best performing version will become the final website.

You can ask if it worths the extra efforts of having two or more different designs.
If your business depends on conversion rates yes, it worths!.
